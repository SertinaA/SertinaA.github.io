About me

Hi! I’m Sertina Augustine Maliyakal.

Lifelong student at heart, most recently completed a double program stint at George Brown College. I know, George Brown and a ton of other colleges and universities in this beautiful city churn out graduates like me by the thousands. So what makes me different? The fact that I’m agile. Or hardworking. Or witty, maybe. Take your pick. Or read on. Your call! But if you stay on, I’ll be glad.

Growing up, I was that kid that meticulously organized her day – complete with jotting down my daily activities in a journal and ticking them off. I also loved taking charge when playing in groups, and planning and plotting for my teams. I was also a great listener and would obsess over finding solutions to any problems my friends or family faced. Today, I realize that that part of me never changed. My work-integrated learning experience as a Project Management Intern perfectly aligned with this side of me and helped me further hone my planning and scheduling skills. I was able to effectively lead my team to complete all our tasks successfully and within budget, and we were thrilled with all the recognition we received for our work. That high is hard to beat, and I look forward to repeating the experience – time and again.

With a *healthy* dose of my perfectionism and optimism and a generous sprinkling of my never-say-die attitude, I’m proud to say that I’ve come a long way. But like Frost once said, I have miles to go yet – and I can’t wait to get started! For a quick lowdown of my abilities, scroll through my projects and certificates below. If you think we can create something great together, I’d love to talk to you!
